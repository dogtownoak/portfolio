Hello, my name is Brandon Ford and this is my portfolio website. I’m a full stack web developer located in the San Francisco Bay Area.

I’ve always been goal oriented and passionate about helping people. Once upon a time I pursued this as an emergency medical technician in San Francisco and Marin. My experiences there led me into helping seniors over 62 achieve financial freedom. I spent 10 years in the financial services industry with one company managing the sales, marketing and operations. Along the road to achieving my goal of becoming the #1 broker in the US I sourced, developed, and implemented many technologies. This is how discovered my love for programming.

The development and implementation of business enhancing software required extensive research, constant learning and the management of software development teams. I know the pains and joys of both implementing and using platform solutions such as Salesforce CRM and building your own solution from the ground up on a mySQL database.

Feel free to explore, download my resume to learn more or fill out the contact form below. Thanks for stopping by.
